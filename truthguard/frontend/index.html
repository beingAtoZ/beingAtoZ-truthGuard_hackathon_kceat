Under the Hood

How TruthGuard Works

A 5-step AI pipeline that goes beyond keywords.

01

Input & Extraction

Text, URLs, or images are parsed. OCR extracts text from images. URLs are scraped for full article content.

02

NLP Preprocessing

spaCy extracts named entities, key claims, sentiment signals, and writing style indicators.

03

Multi-Model Classification

RoBERTa transformer classifies the content. Results are ensemble-weighted for maximum accuracy.

04

Fact Cross-Reference

Key claims are checked against Google Fact Check Tools API and reputable fact-checking databases.

05

LLM Explanation

Gemini AI generates a human-readable explanation — not just a verdict, but the reasoning behind it.
